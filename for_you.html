For Youu

I’ve written posts, made edits, created a page, saved memories, built a website… But the biggest thing I’ve ever done is simple: I loved you — truly, purely, constantly. Even when I was scared. Even when I didn’t know if you remembered me. Even when we were not talking. Even when we were “just friends.” Even when I had no chance… My love never changed. It never reduced. It never faded. Everything I’ve ever created — page, posts, messages — came from one place: pure love. Everything I’ve done — big or small — is just a reflection of how deeply my heart is connected to you. I never did them expecting anything. I did them because loving you makes me feel alive.
