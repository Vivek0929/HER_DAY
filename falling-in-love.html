Falling in love

Falling in love with you was never a moment… it was a feeling that kept growing every single time I saw you. Your eyes, the way they light up when you smile, the little laugh lines when you’re truly happy — those things made my heart fall even deeper without me even knowing. Every time I saw you, I felt like the world paused for a second just to let me breathe you in. And that day you said, “You deserve better”? Better than what, madam? Because for me, there is no “better”. There is only you. From the first day I saw you in that bus in 2017 till this moment, you are the only girl my heart has ever chosen, and the only one it will choose for the rest of my life. You are not someone I want to replace. You are someone I want to protect, cherish, and love forever — exactly as you are.
