Ever wounder - what you mean to me

I know I’m not perfect. I get confused, and I’m still learning what love truly means. But one thing I know for sure is this...I will never leave you. No matter how many years pass… No matter how heavy my heart feels… No matter how much it hurts to know you may never be mine… With every ache, every hope, and every drop of love inside me, I keep falling for you even more. I want to be the person who brings you every happiness in the world. I may not always know how to heal your pain or fix your bad days, but I will stand beside you through all of it— quietly, patiently, lovingly— until you heal. My love is not about being perfect. It is about being loyal. Being present. Choosing you again and again, even when you don’t choose me. Because from the very first moment I saw you… it was always you.
